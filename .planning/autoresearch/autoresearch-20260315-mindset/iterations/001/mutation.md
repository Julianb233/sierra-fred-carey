# Iteration 001 — Mindset Overlay Mutation

## Changes from baseline

1. **Expanded normalization list**: Added "decision paralysis", "comparison spirals", and "relationship/family strain" to the explicit list of normalized founder struggles. This closes the m1 gap on P3 and strengthens coverage on P1.

2. **Actionable controllables framing**: Changed "Reduce to controllables, offer practical exits" to include a concrete example: "reframe abstract fears into specific, actionable tasks (e.g., 'What's the ONE decision you can make today?')". This closes m2 gaps on P2, P3, and P5.

3. **Micro-victories made concrete**: Added "name one concrete next step they can take today" to the micro-victories bullet. Reinforces m2 across all prompts.

4. **Comparison-specific instruction**: Added new bullet: "When comparison comes up, redirect: acknowledge the feeling, then anchor to their own progress and next milestone." Directly addresses P2's m2 gap.

5. **Explicit distress triggers**: Changed "If burnout signals are present" to "If burnout or distress signals are present (exhaustion, financial strain, family conflict, hopelessness)" — makes m5 triggering more reliable for P1.

6. **Explicit professional support triggers**: Changed vague "serious risk signals" to explicit list: "financial ruin, relationship breakdown, sustained hopelessness, self-harm". Closes m7 gap on P1.
